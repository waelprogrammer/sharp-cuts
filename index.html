Our Story

Craft & Character

Sharp Cuts was founded in 2008 with a single mission: to bring old-school barbershop craft into the modern era. What started as a two-chair shop in the heart of downtown has grown into the neighborhood's most trusted destination for precision cuts and genuine style.

"We don't just cut hair — we shape confidence."
— Marcus Reid, Founder

Our team of licensed professionals brings decades of combined experience across classic and contemporary techniques. From fades to tapers, straight-razor shaves to textured styles — every service is delivered with the attention it deserves.
